Frequently asked questions

Can I use this birth plan in a Polish hospital?

Yes. The plan document is generated in Polish so that midwives and doctors in Poland can read and follow it immediately, while the website guides you in English. This is the most practical solution for expats giving birth in Poland.

Is the plan compliant with Polish regulations?

Yes. It follows the Polish Perinatal Care Standard (Journal of Laws 2025, item 1525, effective May 2026), which guarantees rights such as skin-to-skin contact for at least 2 hours, also after a caesarean section.

Immediately after payment through PayHip you receive a download link by email. The file is a print-ready A4 PDF.
